Tatai Waterfall

← Back
Write Your Review

About

Tatai Waterfall is one of Koh Kong's most beautiful waterfalls. It is surrounded by rainforest and crystal-clear water, making it a perfect place for relaxing and taking photos.

Location

Located about 20 km from Koh Kong town. Visitors can travel by motorbike, tuk tuk or private car.

Activities

Photography

Capture beautiful waterfall scenery.

Swimming

Enjoy the natural freshwater.

Nature Walk

Explore the tropical rainforest.

Approximate Spending

  • Entrance Fee: $2
  • Food: $5 - $10
  • Transportation: $10
  • Boat Rental: $8
  • Total Estimated Cost: $20 - $30
