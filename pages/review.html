Tatai Waterfall

Koh Kong, Cambodia 4.5 (120 reviews)

Overview

In the heart of the lush jungles for which Cambodia is famous, a true natural wonder is hidden — the majestic Tatai Waterfall. This site is a breathtaking spectacle of powerful water flows and massive rock cascades, perfect for swimming and a peaceful picnic in the shade.
